History of Our Creation

CEF Navigation was created to make education funding easier to understand for students and families in Ontario. Many people hear about “OSAP” but feel unsure about what it includes, how to apply, what documents are needed, and how grants and loans work together.

Through the CEF Navigation, we focus on navigation and education—breaking down funding pathways into clear steps, practical checklists, and plain-language guidance so learners can move forward with confidence.
